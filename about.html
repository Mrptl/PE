About Prayash Engineering

Established in 2021, we are your "One Stop Solution" for engineering, fabrication, and industrial maintenance.

Who We Are

Prayash Engineering is a dynamic engineering and manufacturing company founded in January 2021. We specialize in delivering end-to-end industrial solutions ranging from heavy mechanical fabrication to precision hydraulic systems.

Our facility in GIDC Savli (Manjusar), Vadodara, is equipped to handle complex projects for diverse sectors including Power, Pharmaceuticals, Automobile, and Heavy Engineering. We pride ourselves on having a team of expert welders and fabricators.

"Quality isn't an act, it is a habit. At Prayash, we ensure every weld, every cut, and every assembly meets the highest industrial standards."
