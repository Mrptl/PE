About Me

Hello! I'm Dev Patel, a 21-year-old passionate Full Stack Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Startup Owner with a keen eye for creating beautiful, functional digital experiences. I love building end-to-end web applications that not only work flawlessly but also delight users with intuitive and engaging interfaces.

As the founder of Suchna Consulting and its subsidiary Suchna Tech & Solutions, I lead a talented team that specializes in web development, graphic designing, UI/UX design, software development, and AI automations. My journey from a curious developer to an entrepreneur has been driven by a passion for transforming ideas into reality—from crafting pixel-perfect designs to architecting robust backend systems that power modern businesses.

When I'm not coding, designing, or strategizing for my ventures, you can find me exploring the latest design trends and development frameworks, contributing to open-source projects, or simply enjoying a good cup of coffee while sketching wireframes for my next creative venture. I believe in continuous learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital space.

My Journey

2021 - Present

Full Stack Development

Started my journey with modern web technologies including React, Node.js, and MongoDB. Built various full-stack applications using the MERN/MEVN stack and mastered both frontend frameworks and backend APIs.

2022 - Present

UI/UX Design & Advanced Frameworks

Expanded into UI/UX design using Figma and Adobe XD, creating user-centered designs with a focus on accessibility and usability. Mastered advanced frameworks like Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS to build production-ready applications.

2023 - 2024

Final Year Project

Developed "SmartCollab Platform", a comprehensive project management and collaboration tool with real-time features, featuring an intuitive UI/UX design and robust full-stack architecture built with Next.js and PostgreSQL.

Future

Continuous Learning

Constantly expanding my skillset now in Deep Learning, NLP, and advanced cloud technologies to build more scalable and intelligent solutions.
